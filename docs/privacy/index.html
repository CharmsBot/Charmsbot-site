Terms of Service Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how CharmsBot ("the Bot," "we," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your data when you interact with it on Discord. By using the Bot, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the Bot.

To provide the Bot's functionality, we collect the following data:

  • Discord User ID: A unique identifier provided by Discord to associate your data with your account, ensuring it is accessible only to you.
  • Character Information: Character name, stats (level, maximum hitpoints, maximum mana, charm points), and selected charms.
  • Hunt Data: Information about killed monsters and session times you provide, plus derived data such as damage calculations or experience estimates.
  • Saved Analyses: Optional names and details of saved hunt analyses or charm configurations, including creature names, charm assignments, and calculated results.

This data is essential for delivering a personalized and functional experience for managing your Tibia characters and analyzing hunts.

We use your data solely to operate the Bot and provide its services, including:

  • Character Management: Enabling you to add, edit, delete, or view your Tibia characters.
  • Hunt Analysis: Processing hunt data to calculate optimal charm assignments, damage estimates, or weapon proficiency XP.
  • Storage of Configurations: Saving and displaying hunt analyses or charm configurations you choose to store.
  • Functionality Improvement: Using interaction data to identify errors and enhance the Bot's performance.

We do not use your data for purposes unrelated to the Bot, such as advertising or external analytics.

Storage: Your data is stored in a secure database, linked to your Discord User ID and accessible only to you.

Retention: We retain your data until you delete it using commands like /character delete or /hunt delete. If the Bot is discontinued, all stored data will be securely deleted.

Security: We implement reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access.

We do not share or sell your data to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Discord API: Your User ID is processed through Discord's API to enable interactions.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal request.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party database providers to store data. These providers only access data necessary for their services.

We are committed to minimizing data sharing and safeguarding your privacy to the fullest extent possible.

You have full control over your data and can:

  • View your data with /character view.
  • Edit your data with /character edit.
  • Delete your data with /character delete (including all associated analyses) or /hunt delete.
  • Stop using the Bot at any time — your data remains secure until you choose to delete it.

The Bot relies on the following third-party services:

  • Discord API: To process commands, send messages, and manage interactions.
  • Database Service: To store and retrieve your character and analysis data.

These services have their own privacy policies, which we recommend you review.

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the Bot's functionality or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be revised. Continued use of the Bot after a change indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
