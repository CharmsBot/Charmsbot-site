🎓 Understanding the Charm System

💎 Charm Points Budget

CharmsBot works within YOUR charm points budget. When you set up your character, you tell the bot your total charm points (found in Cyclopedia → Bosstiary/Charms). The bot then optimizes charm assignments and levels to maximize damage without exceeding your budget.

Charm Level System

All charms can be upgraded to three levels, each with different proc rates and damage multipliers:

  • 🔹 Level 1 — Proc Rate: 5% — Damage Multiplier: 0.5×
  • ✨ Level 2 — Proc Rate: 10% — Damage Multiplier: 1.0×
  • ⭐ Level 3 — Proc Rate: 11% — Damage Multiplier: 1.1×

Damage Calculations

Elemental Charms (Enflame, Freeze, Zap, Poison, Curse, Wound, Divine Wrath):

  • Base: min(5% × creature_health, player_level × 2)
  • Final: base × vulnerability × (1 - mitigation/100)

Physical Charms (Overpower, Overflux):

  • Overpower: min(5% × max_hp, player_level × 2) capped at 8% × creature_health
  • Overflux: min(2.5% × max_mana, player_level × 2) capped at 8% × creature_health
