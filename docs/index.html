Get started in 3 simple steps

1

Create Your Character

Add your character with stats, charm points budget, and unlocked charms with their levels.

Copy your Hunt Analyzer data from Tibia and paste it into the bot. Use /analysis to optimize your current charms or /bestcharms to find the absolute best setup.

3

Get Optimized Results

Receive optimal charm assignments, damage calculations, and element efficiency analysis instantly.

🎓 Understanding the Charm System

💎 Charm Points Budget

CharmsBot works within YOUR charm points budget. When you set up your character, you tell the bot your total charm points (found in Cyclopedia → Bosstiary/Charms). The bot then optimizes charm assignments and levels to maximize damage without exceeding your budget.

⭐ Charm Levels

Each charm can be unlocked at three levels, each with a different proc rate and charm point cost. CharmsBot accounts for all three levels when calculating optimal assignments.

  • 🔹 Level 1 — 5% proc rate
  • ✨ Level 2 — 10% proc rate
  • ⭐ Level 3 — 11% proc rate
